How It Works

A streamlined process to integrate top talent and AI skills into your workflow.

1. Project Scoping

We work with you to define a meaningful 12-week project. This ensures fellows contribute to real business goals while learning from your team.

2. Fellow Matching

Our rigorous vetting process selects the top 1% of student talent. We match you with a fellow whose skills and ambitions align with your project needs.

3. Execution & Growth

Fellows and mentors receive ongoing AI training and support. They execute the project, delivering tangible results and fostering a culture of innovation.

Benefits for Your Business

  • Cost-Effective Talent: Access top-tier, pre-vetted talent for specific projects without the overhead of a full-time hire.
  • AI Upskilling: Your mentors receive cutting-edge AI training, keeping your team ahead of the curve.
  • Leadership Development: Empower your employees to grow as leaders by mentoring the next generation of innovators.
  • Stronger Talent Pipeline: Build relationships with exceptional students, creating a direct pipeline for future recruitment.
